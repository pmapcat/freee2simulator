# Free Estradiol Estimator

**Free Estradiol Estimator** is a simple, browser-based calculator that estimates free estradiol (E2) and free testosterone (T) levels from total E2, total T, and SHBG values.

üîó **Live Demo**: [https://pmapcat.github.io/freee2simulator](https://pmapcat.github.io/freee2simulator)

## ‚ú® Features

- Supports input in both SI and US units
- Converts values automatically between unit systems
- Calculates:
  - Free Estradiol (pmol/L and pg/mL)
  - Free Testosterone (pmol/L and ng/dL)
- Based on the Vermeulen et al. equations  
  [Ref: CEBP 2002](https://cebp.aacrjournals.org/content/11/10/1065)

## üßÆ Scientific Background

The equations used are adapted from:
> Vermeulen A, et al. (2002) _Estimation of free and bound hormone levels in serum_ ‚Äî CEBP Vol. 11(10):1065

These equations consider the equilibrium binding of estradiol and testosterone to albumin and SHBG to estimate free hormone levels.
