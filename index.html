Vermeulen Equations

Free E2 calculation:

fE2 = (E2 - N2 × fE2) / (KsE2 × (SHBG - E2 + N2 × fE2))

where N2 = (KaE2 × Ca) + 1


Free T calculation:

fT = (T - N1 × fT) / (KsT × (SHBG - T + N1 × fT))

where N1 = (KaT × Ca) + 1


Constants:

KaE2 = 4.21×10⁴ L/mol (E2-Albumin)

KsE2 = 3.14×10⁸ L/mol (E2-SHBG)

KaT = 4.06×10⁴ L/mol (T-Albumin)

KsT = 1.0×10⁹ L/mol (T-SHBG)

Ca = Albumin/66500 mol/L (from input)

Solved iteratively to 1×10⁻¹⁵ precision
